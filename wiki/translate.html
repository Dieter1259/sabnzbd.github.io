--- title: Help us translate SABnzbd in your language! ---

Thank you for your interest! Below you can click to our online translation system, hosted by Launchpad.

To start translating, you will need to create an account with Launchpad by clicking Register in the top right.

After you have signed in, you can click on the language you want to translate or click View all languages below the table to see all languages ready to be translated.

NOTE If you click on the number listed for Untranslated, you can directly start by translating the texts that have no existing translation yet. Of course, you are also very welcome to correct existing texts, if worded incorrectly.
