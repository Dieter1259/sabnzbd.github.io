--- title: Release Numbering ---

We use a three group release number.

Major.minor.bugfix

Major A major breaking release. May not be compatible with your current release and may require things like rebuilding your queue. Going back to a previous release is not straightforward.
Minor We added new features. The upgrade will try to preserve your current queue. You may need to review your configuration. Going back should be no problem.
Bugfix We fixed some bugs. Upgrade is trivial. So is going back to an older release (should we have fixed bugs incorrectly or inadvertently added new bugs.

0.7.20 —> 1.0.0
We added lots of features.
You need to rebuild your queue.
You need to review your configuration.

1.0.0 —> 1.0.1
We fixed one or more bugs.
Just install the new release over the old one and continue.
Downgrade: just install 1.0.0 over 1.0.1

1.0.1 —> 1.1.0
We added some features.
Please review the release notes for potential upgrade issues.
